Engineering for Kids!

This app was designed to introduce kids to engineering in a way that they are familiar with: mobile games. It introduces concepts in five areas: mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, programming, web design, and math. The goal is to show kids that engineering is really just like a game, if you play it long enough, eventually you get good at it regardless of how hard it is.
